# Privacy Policy / 隐私政策

**Last Updated / 最后更新日期**: 2026-02-04

---

## English

**TidyDownload** ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our Chrome extension handles your data.

### 1. Data Collection and Usage
**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.**

- **Local Processing**: All file organization and classification logic runs entirely on your local device. No information about your downloads is sent to any external servers.
- **Settings**: Your configuration preferences (rules, language settings) are stored locally in your browser using the `chrome.storage` API and are not shared with anyone.

### 2. Permissions
We request the minimum permissions necessary for the extension to function:

- **`downloads`**: Required to detect new downloads and move them into the appropriate folders based on your rules. We do not read the content of your files.
- **`storage`**: Required to save your custom rules and application settings.

### 3. Third-Party Services
TidyDownload does not use any third-party analytics, tracking tools, or advertising networks.

### 4. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

### 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us via the Chrome Web Store support page.

## 中文 (Chinese)

**TidyDownload**（“我们”）非常重视您的隐私。本隐私政策旨在说明我们的 Chrome 扩展程序如何处理您的数据。

### 1. 数据收集与使用
**我们不会收集、存储或传输您的任何个人数据。**

- **本地处理**：所有的文件整理和分类逻辑完全在您的本地设备上运行。关于您下载文件的任何信息都不会被发送到任何外部服务器。
- **设置存储**：您的配置偏好（如分类规则、语言设置）仅通过 `chrome.storage` API 存储在您的浏览器本地，不会与任何人共享。

### 2. 权限说明
我们仅申请扩展正常运行所需的最小权限：

- **`downloads`（下载管理）**：用于检测新下载的任务，并根据您设定的规则将文件移动到相应的文件夹。我们不会读取您的文件内容。
- **`storage`（存储）**：用于保存您的自定义规则和应用设置。

### 3. 第三方服务
TidyDownload 不使用任何第三方分析工具、追踪服务或广告网络。

### 4. 政策变更
我们可能会不时更新本隐私政策。任何变更都将发布在此页面上。

### 5. 联系我们
如果您对本隐私政策有任何疑问，请通过 Chrome 应用商店的支持页面与我们联系。
